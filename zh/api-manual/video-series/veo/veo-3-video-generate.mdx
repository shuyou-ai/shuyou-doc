> - 模型路径：`google/veo-3.1-generate-001`（Vertex `generateContent`）
> - `generationConfig.responseModalities` 需包含 `VIDEO`
> - 异步任务，需轮询 long-running operation 直至视频就绪
> - 另有：`volcengine/doubao-seedance-1.5-pro`、`volcengine/doubao-seedance-2`
