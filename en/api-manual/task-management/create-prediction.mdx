---

> - Asynchronous job API for third-party and hosted models
> - Returns a `prediction_id` — poll with [Get Prediction](/en/api-manual/task-management/get-prediction)
> - `input` schema varies by model; check the [model catalog](https://shuyou.ai/models)

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create prediction">
    `POST /v1/predictions` with `model` and `input`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Poll until complete">
    `GET /v1/predictions/{prediction_id}` until `status` is `succeeded` or `failed`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use output">
    Read URLs or structured data from the `output` field.
  </Step>
</Steps>
