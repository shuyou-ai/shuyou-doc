> - Model path: `google/veo-3.1-generate-001` (Vertex `generateContent`)
> - Set `generationConfig.responseModalities` to include `VIDEO`
> - Jobs are asynchronous — poll the long-running operation until the video is ready
> - Also available: `volcengine/doubao-seedance-1.5-pro`, `volcengine/doubao-seedance-2`
